    • Why Agile Business Model Innovation (AgileBMI)

  • [Section 5] How can you participate?
    • Most important information to be considered:
      • Set up a team: there should be at least 3 people with 80% of their time available, a sales or PRM role in the team is mandatory!
      • Appoint a mentor
      • Report team members to batch leads
      • Complete the team onepager, and create a OneDrive folder for sharing documents with coaches
      • Prepare and conduct your Maturity Assessment together with a coach
    • Concept preparation phase:
      • Phase 1 lasts 8 weeks with weekly 1:1 coaching session with an IE coach + presentation of weekly deliverables in longer coaching session with multiple teams.
      • The program has a strong focus on the customer problem
      • Your team will have to conduct 100 customer interviews in phase 1
        • Participate in an interview training for preparation
        • Prepare first customer interviews
        • Fill your interview pipeline
      • Start preparing the templates for phase 1
      • Record Timeline & Onepager
