2020 Rutgers Geology Museum Open House

Immersive Exploration of Coastal Communities in a changing Climate - Rutgers Echoes Lab

Since 1968, the Rutgers Geology Museum has hosted an annual Open House and Mineral sale every January. The mineral sale is one of the largest in New Jersey, and has continued to draw hundreds of visitors every year.
